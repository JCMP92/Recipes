Bloody Mary Syringes

Ingredients

  • 2 c. tomato juice
  • 8 oz. vodka
  • 2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp. horseradish
  • 2 tsp. hot sauce (such as Tabasco)
  • Juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. 1. In a large bowl, combine tomato juice, vodka, Worcestershire, horseradish, hot sauce, lemon juice, and pepper. Whisk to combine.
  2. 2. Fill syringes with mixture and chill. Serve cold.
